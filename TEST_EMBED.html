üîÑ Testing the Sync

To test that data syncs correctly:

  1. Open the mobile app in another tab
  2. Go to the "Availability" tab
  3. Tap on any date and set it to "On-Hold" or "Booked"
  4. Wait up to 30 seconds
  5. The calendar below will update automatically!
